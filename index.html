CHAIN OF IDENTITY
Each year you create a new figurine. The plug at the base connects into the socket of the year before — a growing chain of self over time.
↓ PLUG (this year's base)
↑ SOCKET (next year's crown)
Assembly
1. Print each year's figure
2. Insert plug into socket
3. Twist 90° to lock fins
4. Chain grows each year
5. Never break the chain
